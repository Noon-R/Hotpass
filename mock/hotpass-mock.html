Open captures

Hotpass normalizes PIX GPU captures and Nsight GPU traces into one per-pass view. What a capture can show depends on the file — a PIX capture carries occupancy and limiter, a Nsight trace carries occupancy and speed-of-light; missing fields read “—”. For shader, resource and pixel-level work, open the flagged event in PIX.
